Most writing about agentic AI is aimed at people deciding whether to care about it. This site is for the people who have to build it and keep it running.

The plan here is simple. Reference architectures you can reuse. Governance patterns that hold up when a pilot becomes a platform. And honest notes from real builds, including the parts that broke and what it took to fix them.

If you run the platform, you are the audience. More soon.
